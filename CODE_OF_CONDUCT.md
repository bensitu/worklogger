# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want WorkLogger to be a welcoming, respectful, and constructive project for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and patient.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Give feedback that is specific and helpful.
- Focus on the problem, not the person.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or exclusionary language
- Deliberate intimidation or trolling
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and discussions that violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainer through GitHub.
